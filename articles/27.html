Search in topical uses case-insensitive wildcard matching — by which we mean a search for “test” returns matches for “test”, “TEST”, “attest”, “tested”, etc.

Searches are made separately for discussions where the string appears in the title and searches where the string appears in a comment in the discussion. Your results are limited to discussions where you have access either as the owner or as a participant.

Any results found are shown as direct links to the discussion(s) involved.
