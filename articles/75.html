Repair jobs are directly managed in this tool. The first section provides an update tool for the repair job.

options
+email: sets up an email with the repair job as a PDF attachment.
+pdf: creates a PDF version of the repair job for other uses.
+search: switches you to the open repairs tool.
+new: switches you to creating a new repair job.

basic information on the item being repaired.1

NOTES
  1. This acts as a link to the inventory record.
  2. The values are defined by stockd.
  3. Vendors are global entities, managed separately. If the vendor is not connected to this company at present, it's indicated with a *.
  4. The new vendor must have a unique name to your group, or the existing one will be substituted. When a new vendor is created, it's automatically linked to this company, but can be made accessible to other platforms or companies as desired.
