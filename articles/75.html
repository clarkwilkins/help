Repair jobs are directly managed in this tool. The first section provides an update tool for the repair job.

options
+email: sets up an email with the repair job as a PDF attachment.
+pdf: creates a PDF version of the repair job for other uses.
+search: switches you to the open repairs tool.
+new: switches you to creating a new repair job.
