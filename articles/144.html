Credit memos are generated from the original invoice using the button credit memo. Once a credit memo has been generated, it can be updated or deleted from the tools menu. The components of the credit memo are:

ID
A unique id that is shared by the original invoice and the credit memo.
Date
The credit memo date.
Amount
The amount of the invoice credit.
Reason
The reason for the credit.

Creating a credit memo

  1. In the targeted invoice, click on credit memo.
  2. Enter the credit memo date, amount, and reason.
  3. Save the credit memo.

Locating a credit memo

  1. In tools, select credit memos.
  2. Search for the credit memo by customer and/or optional date range.1
  3. Click on the link in memos found to open the credit memo editor.

Deleting a credit memo

  1. Locate the credit memo in memos found.
  2. Use the delete button at the bottom (next to the save button).

Creating a restocking credit

  1. Create the credit memo first, from the original invoice, and include details on the restocking in the credit memo notes.
  2. Now use +apply credits to apply this memo to the same invoice,2 or leave the credit for future use.3

Preview vs. PDF

The credit memo preview (+preview) shows what the generated PDF will look like. The PDF (+pdf) generator makes an actual PDF you can email to clients.


NOTES
  1. The default view is the last 12 months, current month included.
  2. If you have not received payment yet, doing this will reduce the current invoice's open balance by the credit amount.
  3. If you have received payment already, leave this balance unapplied, and reapply it to another invoice balance or do a refund.
