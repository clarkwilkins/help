Invoice search begins by selecting the entity1 and starting and ending dates. The year control is a shortcut to select an entire year.2

You can also filter results down to a single customer.3

Results are delivered with basic information on the invoice and act as direct links to the invoice details.

NOTES
  1. If you are only using one entity, this selector is not shown.
  2. The years available are preset to only include years where the entity has at least one invoice.
  3. The customer list is limited to those who actually have billing in the period.
