The company setup sets preferences specific to your location.

company name
Your company name as it will be displayed on quotes, order, etc.
location for “gone” items
The name used for all items that are now gone from inventory.1
address
Your company address as shown on quotes, sales orders, etc. Three lines maximum.3
contact
Your company contact information as shown on quotes, sales orders, etc. Three lines maximum.3
signature
An optional additional line such as a company slogan or motto that will be added to PDFs.
logo path
A webpage reference to your company logo allows it to be placed on quotes, orders, and other customer-facing documents.2
location for “gone” items
The name used for all items that currently are in an unknown place.1
restock window
How many days from the time of the sale order will you normally accept the items for restock?
new quotes set a followup in X days
Optional. This sets a followup in X business days when a quote is generated.
