The convert button on the sales quote creates a new order with the customer data from the quote. It also establishes an important link between the quote and the order that prevents you from adding something to the order that's not on the quote.

It does not add specific items to the order. That's left to your decisions in the attached items section.

You can generate multiple orders linked to a single originating quote, but you cannot link an order to more than one originating quote.
