Customer refunds require a credit memo with an available (remaining) balance. Begin by going to ☰ > tools, and then select credit memos. Your eligible credit memos will appear in open credit balances.1

  1. Click on the desired memo.
  2. In the preset check screen that shows up, you can change the check number,2 date, checking account and memo field. You can also change the amount.3
  3. Post the check.

What this check does is:

  • lower your cash balance by the check amount.
  • lower the available credit by the same amount.
  • create the liability check, payable to your client.4

NOTES
  1. If this section is not present, you have nothing eligible for a refund, and a message to that effect should be present.
  2. The default is “TBD”.
  3. Avoid going over the available balance on the memo. This creates functional errors in your balance sheet that can only be corrected by making this amount no more than the available credit remaining on this memo.
  4. This can be modified after the check is created.
