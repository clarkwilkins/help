The attached items section shows items on the order in some detail.

qty
Normally, the quantity is set to one, because it's usually a discrete item.
rate
This is always shown as rate 1 (exchange price).1
item details
Details about the item and the rate 2 price are shown here.2
+quote xxxx
This is a link to the original sales quote.
+item
Opens the tool to add another item to the order.3
NOTES
  1. In an outright sale, exchange and outright prices are the same. Otherwise, orders assume the exchange credit will be used, and they show the lower price.
  2. The rate 2 price is shown to the customer as “rebilling rate”. If the sale is outright, this is not included in the customer version.
  3. Subject to the same restrictions as discussed here.
