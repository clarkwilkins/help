A customer account can link an unlimited number of clients. By default, these contact are available in all locations, but you can do some filtering on email contact lists.

name
This is a required field.1
company
This is a required field.1
title, email1, email2, phone1, phone 2
Optional fields2
Use contact on these locations
Associates the contact with a designated location. Multiple contact-location associations are supported.
grant access to accountd
The contact is accessible in normal accountd operations.3
grant access to servicd
The contact is accessible in normal servicd operations.3
grant access to stockd
The contact is accessible in normal stockd operations.3
notes
Notes about the contact are optional, and may be visible to regular users depending on the tool.
tags
Tags about the location are optional, and may be visible to regular users depending on the tool.

NOTES
  1. A client record must have a name or company. You can use both.
  2. Email addresses specified here become available in the email setup view.
  3. All clients are accessible within a company ID (servicd, stockd) or an entities group (accountd) if this is checked. However, a user with access to manage clients can still see these groups within customer management, because this tool exists above the individual platforms.
