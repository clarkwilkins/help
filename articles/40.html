Email setup in stockd allows you to send the associated information to any valid email address. The default settings for to/cc/bcc are determined by the client-side email setup as explained here. You can modify these as needed. If your view is not current showing "cc" or "bcc" options, use +cc and +bcc to bring them into view.1

subject is a required field which is usually preset by stockd, but you can change it at will.

text is an optional field where you can put your comments as the main body of the email message.

Depending on the source of the email, different attachments are included as follows:

source
attachments
quote
The sales quote and current2 terms and conditions are sent as two PDF attachments.
order
The sales order is attached as a PDF.
returns
A list of associated return items and status is attached as a PDF.
toolkits
A toolkit contents list is attached as a PDF.
repairs
A repair job report is attached as a PDF.

NOTES
  1. If the default email settings for cc or bcc are not set on the master record, they are hidden by default.
  2. The most recent terms used with this quote.
