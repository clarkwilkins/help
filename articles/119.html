The email manager is triggered from a supported item1 using the +email shortcut. The purpose is to help you set up and send a structured email that is connected back to the item where this started.

The content of the mail is preset based on the notice type you select, but is completely modifiable.2 Upon selecting a notice type, your preset content is shown for further editing.

+repairs
returns you to the open repair jobs view
+notices
resets this view

Any unsent notices will appear here as pending notice emails. Click on one to send, update, or delete it.

NOTES
  1. As of 2020.06.04, it's only implemented for repair jobs.
  2. The subject is not modifiable as of v2.22.
