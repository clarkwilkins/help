Standard parts name are assigned to SKUs to make the part name display consistent. When you enter a new inventory item or parts request, this is the part name that's automatically set. In some cases, a user may designate a part name which is incorrect, or perhaps inconsistent with your company standards. You can make the changes needed here using +edit.

Change the SKU and/or name as needed and update it. All related items are changed immediately.

+CAPS is a convenient shortcut if you prefer all capitals in your part names.
