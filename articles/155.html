There are two purposes for this report: to show you all customer credit memos, and to show you how credits are handled, and where the credits go. The report is accessible at analysis:credit memo usage.

Each customer account which has at least one credit memo is shown.1 The results are sorted by date to show three types of actions, and their effect on the client's available credit balance.

First, it shows any credit memos posted on the day.2 These increase the credit balance.

Second, it shows any customer refunds issued.3 These decrease the credit balance.

Third, it shows any credit that was applied to an invoice balance on this day.4 These also decrease the credit balance.


NOTES
  1. If you are running more than one set of company accounts, the entity selector will appears to let you switch “books”.
  2. Clicking on the line will open the credit memo.
  3. Clicking on this line will open the refund check.
  4. Clicking on this tool will open the payment tool in credit mode with this transaction loaded.
