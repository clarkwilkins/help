Simplexable operates three primary platforms that support “global users”.

accountd
Full accounting services and financial analysis with ability to manage multiple companies in a single application/login.
servicd
Service and job management.
stockd
Inventory, warehouse, and parts sales management.

A global user can access any of the platforms as configured in their settings.

Once Simplexable assigns you a new company ID with pro access, you can begin with one platform, and add the others at will. Login is a single event — one login for access to all allowed areas.

When you make a platform on company X available to a user, it adds to the list of environments where they can work. Each user/company/platform is a unique set of access privileges, but the user is able to move from one to another at will using the ☰ menu option switch platforms.
