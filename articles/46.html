SKU trend analysis runs automatically on all inventory search results. It generates a results table, aggregated by year, of quotes and orders for each unique SKU found in the results.

Consider the following factors for setting pricing:

  • Is there a high demand for quotes on this item (market demand)?
  • Are you converting a meaningful fraction of quotes? If not, do you see price reductions in the data? Do lower prices result in more orders?
  • Where quote or order rates are lower, are you getting the sales?
  • Have you quoted this item to this client before? Did they buy it?
