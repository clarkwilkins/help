The activity by date chart shows the number of user activities1 recorded from start to end per the setup. You can use this as a rough gauge of the overall information flow during the period. Results are aggregated by date.

The activity by category report aggregates the activity by general category.2

NOTES
  1. Only significant activities such as adding an item are record. An action can make multiple trips to the servers during execution, but the whole of these trips is counted as a single event.
  2. General categories are groupd by stockd per the primary nature of the action.
