Followups are general purpose reminders that the target item needs to be reviewed and/or acted on at a future date. When a followup becomes due, it appears on the ☰ menu, and it turns red once it becomes overdue. The idea here is to provide a simple notification system that helps your team avoid overlooking things.

The followup has these components:

target
The item this followup is concerned with.
date
When the followup will become active.
assigned
This is an option to assign a followup to a specific user.
notes
An optional field to add context on next steps, etc.

+assign: this shows or hides the assign followup area. If the followup has been assigned, the area is visible by default.

+notes: this shows or hides the followup notes area. If the followup has notes, the area is visible by default.

Many tools provide shortcuts to clear a followup, but all followup interfaces have the clear function on the date picker view as well. In some cases, such as a sales quote that becomes inactive, the followup is removed automatically.
