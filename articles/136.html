Followups are scheduled reminders to come back to a job ticket or a task. You can set them from the ticket update view or the task view.

In addition to the date, you can also assign a followup to a specific user. When you open followups due on the ☰ menu, there are two numbers (X/Y). X is the number of followups due today. Y is the number of followups that are overdue. Your count and list will not contain any followups assigned to another user.
