The Simplexable login service has two methods of login: direct and username/password.

Direct login links are obtained by entering your registered email or phone number. If a match is found, you are sent an email with a direct login link. We recommend bookmarking this somewhere safe for your convenience.

To use a username/password login, follow these steps:

  1. Go to preferences via ☰: other tools: setup (personal preferences)
  2. In new login, enter a new username and password.
  3. Click the save button.

Your login information is encrypted and saved.1 There's an easy recovery path if you forget it though.

  1. Use your registered email or phone to get a login link emailed to you.
  2. Login via the direct link from step 1.
  3. Update your username/password with the procedure above.
NOTES
  1. We can't look it up for you. It's encrypted before saving.
