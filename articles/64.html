Documents are stored in your private online repository.1 Your users are the only ones allowed to maintain document records.

Documents are accessible from the following tools (and can multiply link to these items):

  • inventory items
  • sales orders
  • sales quotes
  • repair jobs

To add a document:

  1. Click on +new in the documents section.
  2. Choose a file to upload or take a picture with your mobile device.
  3. Update the description as needed.
  4. Set public access allowed to no if you don't want this document in normal public views.

To edit a document:

  1. Click on +edit in the desired document's caption.
  2. Update the caption, notes or public field as needed.

To remove a document connection:

  1. Click on +edit in the desired document's caption.
  2. Press -unlink next to the item that needs to be disconnected.

To add a document connection:

  1. Click on +edit in the desired document's caption.
  2. Enter search terms.
  3. Find the targeted item in the search results and click +link to connect it.

A single document can link to an unlimited number of items.

NOTES
  1. Actual storage is done on Amazon S3. Your files are segregated in a folder tied to your unique company ID, and they are only accessible if you give out a specific document's link address.
