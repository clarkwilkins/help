This tool is used for batch verification of inventory

  1. Go to the physical location.
  2. Open the location contents in this tool.
  3. Strongly recommended: remove all items from the location temporarily.
  4. Click on each item as you put it back. This marks the item as verified today, by you.
  5. Attend to any missing items or items that were expected. (See below.)

Missing items are best marked as lost and not ok to sell.

Misplaced items should either be returned to their proper location and verified, or have their location updated to current status.
