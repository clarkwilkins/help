When starting a new quote, your customer accounts list is restricted to clients that (a) are registered for your current company and (b) are enabled to access stockd.

If you don't see the client you need, select +new client and either create the client or find it,1 and select the checkbox “grant access to stockd”.2

Once you have selected a customer, the quote can be created.

NOTES
  1. A client that is on the customer list, but marked “inaccessible”, is one that does not have access to stockd at this time.
  2. For information on client management, click here.
