This section handles preferences that are specific to stockd. The user settings at the top affect all platforms.

show tips
This turns on/off the user tips option. When set to on, you are shown any tips relevant to the current tool being displayed. Tips are individually suppressed by clicking “got it”, or turned off altogether by unchecking this option.
show open tasks
When checked, if you are assigned any currently open tasks, a menu option appears to indicate this and provide a shortcut to the listing of your open tasks. This option does not appear on the menu until the next page is loaded.
timezone options
This option converts all displayed times to your local timezone.
