Managing an existing vendor begins with selecting them in setup. This brings up the editor.

name
All vendor records must have a name.
platform accessibility
A checkbox appears here for each company defined in your group.1 Add checks for the company/companies where you intend to use this vendor.
notes
Use this as desired — for account numbers is one possible use.

When deleting a vendor, you are asked to confirm, as this is not reversible.

NOTES
  1. Additional companies are instantiated by Simplexable on request. There's no charge for this service.
