Quote search allows location of quotes by a variety of search filters.

+active
Show all currently active quotes.
+last 7 days
Show all quotes created in the last week.
customer
Filters the results by customer account.1
active/inactive
Filters on the quote state.
name/part number/item number
Finds quote with items that wild-card match (name, part number) or exact-match (item number).
notes
Extends the wild-card match to include notes.

Click on any quote in the results to get further details.

NOTES
  1. Run this filter by itself to get all quotes for one account.
