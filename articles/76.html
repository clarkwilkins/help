A new repair is started by specifying an item.

  1. Initiate the search and select from the eligible items.1,2
  2. Select the repair type and priority.
  3. Add optional (but recommended) notes.
  4. Initiate the job.
NOTES
  1. Items are eligible for repair if they are (a) not in good condition, (b) in stock, and (c) not on a repair job already.
  2. TIP: to see all eligible items at once, enter % in the find items input box.
