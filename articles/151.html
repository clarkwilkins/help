Each entity needs an Adjustments and Refunds account1 to be able to process credit memos. When a credit memo is created, it results in a journal entry from the relevant Sales Income account to this one.2 For this reason, the account is required before you begin using credit memos.

Follow these steps:

  • Go to ☰ chart of accounts.
  • Click on +new in the entity/company that needs this account.
  • Create the account1 with type Income.
  • Go to ☰ tools and select the option special accounts.
  • Select this entity if necessary, and then set adjustments and refunds account to your new account.

After completing these steps, your credit memo function should operate properly.

NOTES
  1. The standard account is 399.000: Adjustments and Refunds.
  2. Both of these accounts have the type Income. This is, in fact, a lateral transfer that does not affect the balance sheet until a refund is processed.
