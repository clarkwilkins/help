The details of the email are standard.

SUBJ
The email subject as sent.
FROM
The sender as displayed on the sent email.1
TO/CC/BCC
recipients are shown as was set up on the email.
text
The email body as sent.
sent by
The stockd user who sent the mail and when it was sent.2
NOTES
  1. It's possible to create an email with an alternate sender address.
  2. stockd allows setup of an email for send at a future time.
