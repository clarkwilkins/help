Your account settings have the following controls:

owner settings
setting
effect
name
Your name as it will be displayed in discussions. This is a mandatory field.
email 1
Your email. This is a mandatory field that must be a valid email address.
email 2
Your optional secondary email. This is a mandatory field that must be a valid email address.
sms 11
Your mobile number. This is a mandatory field.
sms 21
Your optional secondary phone.
alert settings2
Configures how you receive notifications of topic updates.
preferred color
This sets your preference for the background color for your own comments. See this article for more details.
active status
Configures the status of your account. When inactive, you cannot post to or create topics.
delete my account and data

Use of this button removes all traces of your account — comments, topics, everything. It's subject to confirmation. If you just want to take a break, set your account to inactive instead.

active and blocked contacts

By default, when a contact is created, both sides get an active contact record and can invite each other to topics. If you don't want the contact to be able to invite you or to avoid having them show up on your available list, you can block them here. Clicking moves the contact from one list to the other.

NOTES
  1. 10-digit minimum.
  2. The setting here is overridden if you configure a specific discussion. See discussion settings
