Repair notices have seven types as determined in notice on the +setup screen.

  • Sending part to repair vendor
  • Requesting status of repair
  • Requesting billing information
  • Modification of repair request/terms
  • Requesting shipment of item(s)
  • Requesting documentation
  • Cancelling repair

The email text is provided as a template which is completely modifiable in each email.1 The mail message will automatically include a PDF of the repair order for the recipients' reference. There are multiple other fields which change their labeling depending on the notice type.

+setup
returns you to the repair notices manager
+email
is used to send the notice now
+job...
returns you to the repair job

email options

This section lets you set up an email for delivery in the future.

send date/time
sets the date and time when the email will be sent
sender
customize the “from” (sender) address
to/cc/bcc
recipients are entered one per line as name:email, email:name, or just email.
save email (draft)
for an email that's not ready just yet
delete email
deletes the draft
NOTES
  1. If you have a previous notice of this type saved for this item, it will be loaded here. However, each sent notice is saved separately and linked back to the repair job.
  2. For example, the repair status request has “scope of work” and “additional notes”.
