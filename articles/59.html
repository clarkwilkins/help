Cost sources are collections of one or more cost records that aggregate to define the cost of a specific item. There are three types.

source expense
A single cost that acquired a single item.
source job
A collection of costs (usually a repair job) that acquired a single item.
source group
A collection of costs that acquired a group of item collectively.

stockd allows for the creation and management of unlimited cost sources as records that help you to understand the sunk costs associated with items in your inventory. This can be critical information for pricing sales quotes, and it helps you to get to truth in your inventory accounting as well.
