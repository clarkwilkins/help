The default mode for job search runs a case-insensitive search on job description, work performed, and notes.1

You can access additional search options via +filters.

assigned user
Only show tickets assigned to the selected user.
customers + locations
Only show tickets assigned to a specific customer with the option to filter down to a single location.2
active/inactive
This filters tickets by the job status. Level 1 and 2 tickets are active (not started or in progress). Level 3-5 tickets are inactive (incomplete, complete, or canceled).
recent tickets
Presets to show tickets from the last 7/30/90 days, or the last year.

The results are ordered by the job number, and each row is a link to the ticket.

NOTES
  1. Not to be confused with regular notes. This is the field shown just below work performed.
  2. Locations are optional.
