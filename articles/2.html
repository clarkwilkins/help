The customer payments report is accessed from analysis and intended to provide system-wide search capability for payments. First, you select an entity to trigger the report.

The default mode is to show all payments. You can further filter this down using the customer control and/or the starting and ending dates.

entity
The initial selection must be the entity. This determines which customer accounts are available.
customer
All accounts available from the selected entity are available here. Inactive accounts (if any) are followed by a *.
starting date1
Optional control to show payments posted on or after this date.
ending date1
Optional control to show payments posted on or before this date.

When results are found, a three-column report is displayed.

date1,2
The posting date of the payment.
amount2
The amount of the payment.
details3
This section has a number of items related to the invoice:
  • invoice number
  • customer name
  • original invoice date
  • remaining balance4

NOTES
  1. The search runs based on the posting date which may differ from the recorded payment date.
  2. These columns serve as a link to the payment details.
  3. This column serves as a link to the invoice details.
  4. Since the original invoice can have payments outside the starting-ending window, the balance here is showing the effect of all payments on the invoice and is not affected by any date windowing here.
