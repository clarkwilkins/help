Initially a toolkit has no items in it. You can add anything in your inventory.1

options
+email: send an email with the toolkit as an attachment
+pdf: create a toolkit PDF
+new: create a new toolkit
+setup: switches to the kit setup view

To add an item to the kit, enter its item number in new item. If the selection is in stock, and is not already defined as a toolkit itself, you can click on it to add to the kit.

To remove an item, click on it and confirm the removal.

NOTES
  1. When an item is added to the kit, it becomes a follower of the kit item.
