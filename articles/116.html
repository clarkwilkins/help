The parts request breakdown looks at parts trends in three ranges: the last 30 days, the last 90 days, and all recorded requests. All clients are charted, subject to a threshold.1

Clicking on a chart will display (or hide) the underlying data for all clients on the chart.

NOTES
  1. The 90-day and all request charts dump everyone with < 1% of the total into “Other”, but you can see this in the details.
