Consumables are tracked materials that are used for production and/or business operations. They are considered inputs, and reporting is generated for the purpose of maintaining proper stock levels. These items are not listed or shown in standard stockd operations.

name
The name of the consumable ( i.e. “cases” or “dials”).
sku/part number
An identifier unique to this consumable type.2
on hand
How many of this item are in stock?
on order
How many of this item are on order?
target qty
What is considered a desirable stock level?
notes
optional.
NOTES
  1. A unique name for each consumable type being tracked is highly recommended to avoid confusion.
  2. Each consumable type must have a unique SKU.
