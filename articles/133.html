This view is used to set up the rate sheet for your billing staff. You can switch here via +billing. This is the billing view for this ticket — not for the entire job.

Labor and travel are divided into regular, overtime, and doubletime hours (rate 1-3). servicd makes no attempt to determine these divisions — or how much you bill. These fields are at your sole discretion.

Costs is treated as a single sum billed costs, but you can offer a breakdown for the client via costs notes.
