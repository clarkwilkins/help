The open repair jobs report is based on the current repair condition set on the job. The results are ordered to show the most recently opened jobs first.

+filters: shows inputs that will search for a specific repair job and/or filter the current results.

+reset: clears all filters and returns to the default report state.

+new: starts a new repair job.

+update: open an area on a repair job for a quick update of the repair status or priority without having to go into the details.
