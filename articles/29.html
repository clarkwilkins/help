This analysis simply compares what the selected entity billed in the period against what it expensed. No account is taken here for actual receipts or payments, as that's the role of cash position analysis.1 The analysis also accounts for categorized journal entries.2

The chart aggregates by month, but is bound by the starting and ending dates in setup. Any month which has no income data is set to zero. The same applies on the expense line. net = billed - expenses which is why the green line can go negative.

NOTES
  1. See the daily cash position chart.
  2. Be careful to properly set categories when journaling costs or income, but also be careful to not set categories when journaling from one income account to another, or from one expense account to another.
