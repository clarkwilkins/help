When adding items, stockd restricts your choices.

  • The item SKU must be on the original quote.
  • The item must be in stock.
  • The item must not be on this order yet.

The items shown contain details to help you choose the best item for your order. Some consideration should be given not only to item condition and location, but also to the current value. If you're maintaining a disciplined approach to cost sources, it's prudent to sell the most “costly” item first.
